Games

I started making video games in 2020 and I'm in love with the entire process. I mainly do art, animation and game design, but I dabble in music and sound design as well!

The 2020 Trail

BONUS: Check out this clip of one of our players enjoying the game. It's a hoot!

The 2020 Trail is the one game I've shipped so far. I made it with my husband in 2020-21 before our kids arrived and derailed our indie dev dreams. It holds a 91% positive rating on Steam, which I'm very proud of. I'd love to make another one someday — and I keep an eye out for opportunities where game design experience is relevant.

You can check out more details, see player reviews, and play the game for free on Steam!
