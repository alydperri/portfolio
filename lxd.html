GemQuest Card Game
Games
Robbins Brothers · 2014

GemQuest Card Game

A simple 2-player card game to help new sales associates practice the Robbins Brothers selling system and overcome client objections. Low-fi, high engagement.

One player takes the role of "client" and deals objection cards. The other player responds with cards representing points in the selling system. Wrong response? Take both cards. Right response? Client takes both. First to run out of cards wins.
